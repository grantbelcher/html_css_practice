Watercross can be simply put as motocross on Personal Watercrafts (PWCs or jetskis). The exception being, these athletes compete on unpredictable liquid track varying from oceans, lakes and rivers. Our team challenges the notion of what is possible on a PWC

Closed Course

Similar to motocross on the water, closed course racing is handle bar-to-handle bar competition that pits rider and machine in a personal watercraft battlefield. Reaching top speeds of over 90 mph; closed course racing features a buoy-marked, 1/2- to 3/4-mile track that is designed to keep the competition tight and fans close to the action.

Freestyle

Each event features a Freestyle segment that highlights the acrobatical abilities of these highly specialized athletes and their watercraft. One of the truly “extreme sports,” freestyle features some of the most gifted athletes on the beach, performing two-minute routines in an Olympic-style format for a panel of judges.

Recreational Events

Pro Watercross Recreational include fun, family friendly, adventure and community events for all types of PWCs and riders of all ages and skill levels, while also promoting overall PWC safety, responsibility, environmental stewardship and a positive image of the PWC community."
